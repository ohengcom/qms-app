Why clear cache?
  • See the latest version after deployment
  • Fix Service Worker redirect issues
  • Resolve authentication problems
  • Clear stale data

Quick Actions:

Manual Steps (if needed):
  1. Open DevTools (F12)
  2. Go to Application tab
  3. Click "Clear storage"
  4. Check all boxes
  5. Click "Clear site data"
  6. Hard refresh (Ctrl+Shift+R or Cmd+Shift+R)
